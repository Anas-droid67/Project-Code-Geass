Battles & History

1. Battle of Shinjuku

Date: a.t.b. 2017

Winner: Zero & Resistance Forces

Location: Shinjuku Ghetto

How was it won?

Lelouch used Geass for the first time, turning Britannian soldiers against one another.

Impact

The birth of Zero and the beginning of the rebellion.

2. Battle of Saitama Ghetto

Date: a.t.b. 2017

Winner: Black Knights

Location: Saitama Ghetto

How was it won?

Zero coordinated resistance forces using superior tactics.

Impact

The Black Knights gained popularity among the Elevens.

3. Battle of Narita

Date: a.t.b. 2017

Winner: Black Knights

Location: Narita Mountains

How was it won?

Zero triggered a massive landslide that devastated Cornelia's forces.

Impact

The first major victory against Britannia.

4. Battle of Kawaguchi Lake

Winner: Britannia

Location: Lake Kawaguchi

How was it won?

Britannian forces overwhelmed resistance positions.

Impact

Forced the Black Knights to regroup.

5. Battle of Tokyo Settlement

Winner: Britannia

Location: Tokyo Settlement

How was it won?

Chaos caused by the Euphemia Incident shifted the battle.

Impact

Led directly to the Black Rebellion.

6. Black Rebellion

Winner: Britannia

How was it won?

The Black Knights lost leadership and coordination.

Impact

Zero disappeared and the resistance collapsed temporarily.

7. Operation Liberation of Japan

Winner: Black Knights

How was it won?

Zero returned and launched a large-scale offensive.

Impact

The Black Knights were reborn as a major military force.

8. Second Battle of Tokyo Settlement

Winner: Black Knights

How was it won?

Advanced Knightmare Frames and superior planning.

Impact

Britannia's hold over Area 11 weakened significantly.

9. Battle of the Chinese Federation

Winner: Black Knights

How was it won?

Political alliances and military intervention.

Impact

The United Federation of Nations gained a powerful ally.

10. Battle of the Valkyrie Squadron

Winner: Britannia

How was it won?

The Knights of the Round dominated the battlefield.

Impact

Showed the strength of Britannia's elite pilots.

11. Black Knights Coup

Winner: Black Knights Leadership

How was it won?

The Black Knights turned against Zero.

Impact

Lelouch lost control of his army.

12. Battle of Mount Fuji

Winner: Britannia

How was it won?

Britannian forces countered the UFN offensive.

Impact

Delayed the collapse of Britannian influence.

13. Battle of Tokyo

Winner: Britannia

How was it won?

Schneizel deployed overwhelming military power.

Impact

Marked the beginning of the final conflict.

Final War

14. Battle of Damocles

Winner: Lelouch's Forces

How was it won?

Damocles was captured after an intense aerial battle.

Impact

The world was freed from FLEIJA domination.

15. Lelouch vs Schneizel

Winner: Lelouch

How was it won?

Lelouch used Geass to defeat Schneizel politically.

Impact

The final obstacle to world peace was removed.

16. Zero Requiem

Winner: The World

How was it won?

Lelouch sacrificed himself so that humanity could unite against a common enemy.

Impact

The end of the Britannian Empire and the beginning of a new era of peace.
